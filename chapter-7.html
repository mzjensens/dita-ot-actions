Chapter 7: The Name Revealed

The Final Guess

On the third day, the little man returned, eager to hear the queen’s final attempt. She watched him closely, hiding the confidence that had come with knowing his name. She pretended to ponder, listing a few more names that she knew were wrong, as he watched with a grin. Finally, she spoke the name “Rumpelstiltskin,” and in that instant, his smile disappeared, realizing that she had outwitted him.

Rumpelstiltskin's Rage

The little man was very, very angry, unable to believe that his secret had been discovered. He stomped and shrieked, his face contorted with rage. In a final act of fury, he disappeared, leaving the queen alone with her child.

Peace Restored

With the little man gone, the queen embraced her child, feeling an immense relief and joy that her bargain had been broken. She had outsmarted the strange little man, and her family was safe once more. Life in the kingdom returned to peace, and the queen never forgot the lesson learned from her encounter with Rumpelstiltskin.
